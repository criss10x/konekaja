FAQ

Before you ask

Do I have to send crypto before receiving the cash?

Never. We run a fiat-first protocol: the agent counts the cash in front of you, you hold and verify it, and only then do you send the crypto wallet-to-wallet. You are never exposed.

How fast can I get cash?

WhatsApp response takes under 5 minutes. Agent arrival: 20–40 minutes in South Bali (Canggu, Seminyak, Kuta, Jimbaran, Nusa Dua, Uluwatu), 30–50 minutes in Central Bali (Denpasar, Sanur, Ubud), and 40–65 minutes for remote areas.

Which cryptocurrencies and networks do you accept?

All crypto with liquidity — BTC, ETH, USDT, USDC, SOL, BNB, XRP, TON and more — across 15+ networks including Bitcoin, ERC-20, TRC-20, BEP-20, Solana, Polygon, Arbitrum, and Base. Not sure about yours? Just ask on WhatsApp.

What are the minimum and maximum trade sizes?

From Rp 1,000,000 up to Rp 2,000,000,000 per transaction. High-value trades are secured with multi-sig wallets and handled discreetly.

Are there any fees on top of the quoted rate?

No. The rate our team quotes on WhatsApp is the final, all-in price — delivery included. No hidden fees, no surprises at handover.

Do I need to create an account or fill out forms?

No account, no long forms, no paperwork. Privacy is part of the service — the verification burden sits on our agents, not on you.

When are you open?

Every day from 08:00 to 22:00 WITA — including weekends and public holidays.
